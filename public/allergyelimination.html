What is A/SERT?

A/SERT (Allergy / Sensitivity Elimination and Reprogramming Technique) is a revolutionary treatment system based on the principles of Chiropractic, Acupuncture, and Applied Kinesiology (Muscle Response Testing). This technique was developed by Dr. John Brimhall, and is a modification of NAET (Nambudripads Allergy Elimination Technique) developed by Dr. Devi Nambudripad. This treatment, in conjunction with the 6 Steps To Wellness Protocol, can bring about dramatic improvement -at times even total elimination- for all types of allergies. It is simple, non-invasive and extremely effective. It is currently practiced by hundreds of health practitioners, including MDs, nurses, acupuncturists, chiropractors, dentists, and, to some extent, allergy sufferers themselves.
